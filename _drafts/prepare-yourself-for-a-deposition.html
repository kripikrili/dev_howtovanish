Having your deposition taken is horrible.  It means you are either involved in a lawsuit or you are a witness.    Most people don't wake up in the morning hoping for that.

Depos can be long and very boring.

If you are involved in the suit, you are probably being deposed to probe your case for weaknesses.  I doubt you ask for that for Christmas.

There may be a lot of personal or business information that you don't want to reveal.  Either it is embarrassing, or, it could be damaging to your case.

But, that information is only going to come out if you volunteer it or if the opposing attorney asks for it.
